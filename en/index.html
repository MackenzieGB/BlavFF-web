What is BlavFF?

BlavFF is a mini festival for the furry, therian and cosplay community, organized by Furosion o.z. It was created because Slovakia lacked an event of this type for young people, which is why it is intended for people under 30.

The event is intended for people under 30. Exceptions apply for legal guardians, speakers, or partners.
